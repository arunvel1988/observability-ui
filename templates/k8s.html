Observability in Kubernetes - LGTM Stack

The LGTM Stack is a modern observability solution for Kubernetes. It helps you monitor your applications and infrastructure using Logs, Metrics, and Traces.

LGTM stands for Loki (Logs), Grafana (Visualization), Tempo (Traces), and Mimir (Metrics).

Why LGTM for Kubernetes?

  • Unified view of logs, metrics, and traces for your workloads.
  • Seamless integration with Prometheus and Kubernetes metrics.
  • Scalable, open-source, and cloud-native solution.
  • Easier troubleshooting and faster root cause analysis.

LGTM Components

  • Loki – Collects and stores logs from Kubernetes pods.
  • Mimir – Stores and queries metrics from Prometheus.
  • Tempo – Enables distributed tracing for microservices.
  • Grafana – Visualizes logs, metrics, and traces in one unified dashboard.
